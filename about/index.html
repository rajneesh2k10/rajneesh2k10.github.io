About Me

I’ve been a software engineer for over a decade. My love for programming began long before my career started. My professional journey started with Amazon in 2012, where I spent ten transformative years. Currently, I’m a part of Uber, continuing to tackle exciting challenges in the world of databases.

This blog stems from a mix of curiosity and a desire to document the cool things I encounter — things that often slip through the cracks of a busy workday. Whether it’s a fascinating concept I stumbled upon or a fun project I brought to life, I wanted a space to keep track of it all and share it with the world. I hope you find something here that sparks your curiosity too!
